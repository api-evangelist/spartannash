A full-store remodel reflects a renewed commitment to the loyal shoppers who built the store’s local legacy PAW PAW, Mich. — March 23, 2026 — The Paw Paw Family Fare ® has completed a highly-anticipated full-store transformation, bringing expanded fresh offerings and refreshed departments to one of the community’s most established neighborhood destinations. The remodel focuses on the areas of the store shoppers care about most, including expanded produce, an upgraded hot bar experience, expanded beer, wine and liquor selections and a nostalgic candy and soda section that celebrates longtime favorites.
