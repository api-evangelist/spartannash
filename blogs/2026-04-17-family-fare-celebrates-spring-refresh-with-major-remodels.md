Three refreshed stores in Pennfield, Marshall and Albion bring modern upgrades to the shopping experience, brightening aisles and baskets for store guests CALHOUN COUNTY, Mich. — April 17, 2026 — As shoppers refresh their routines and carts this spring, Family Fare ® has completed its own series of refreshes — celebrating three store remodels across Calhoun County. These remodels at the Pennfield, Marshall and Albion locations align in-store upgrades with Family Fare’s broader Spring Refresh campaign, bringing modern upgrades to the in-store and online shopping experience.
